Paracetamol
Dose: 1g PO/IV every 4-6 hrs (Max 4g/24h)
Mild to moderate pain, pyrexia. Caution: Low weight (<50kg).

Dihydrocodeine
Dose: 30mg PO every 4-6 hrs (Max 180mg/24h)
Moderate pain (postnatal). Avoid in breastfeeding if baby premature/apnoeic.

Pethidine
Dose: 50-100mg IM
1st stage labour. Crosses placenta; may cause neonatal resp depression / drowsiness.
Analgesia
Entonox
Dose: Inhaled 50:50 O2/N2O
Self-administered. Rapid onset/offset. SE: Nausea, lightheadedness.

Syntocinon (Oxytocin) - 3rd Stage
Dose: 10 IU IM or 5 IU IV (Slow)
Active management of 3rd stage. Prevention/Treatment of PPH.
3rd Stage
Syntometrine
Dose: 1ml IM (5 IU Oxy + 500mcg Ergo)
3rd Stage. Contraindicated in Hypertension/Pre-eclampsia. SE: Nausea/Vomiting.
3rd Stage
Ergometrine
Dose: 500mcg IM or 250mcg IV (Emergency)
PPH Management. Causes sustained tetanic contraction. C/I: Hypertension.
PPH
Carboprost (Haemobate)
Dose: 250mcg Deep IM (Rep q15m, Max 8 doses)
Severe PPH. C/I: Asthma (causes bronchospasm).
PPH
Misoprostol
Dose: PPH: 800mcg (Rectal/Sublingual). IOL: Varies (e.g. 25mcg).
Prostaglandin analogue. Used for IOL or PPH when oxytocics unavailable/failed.

Labetalol

Nifedipine (Modified Release)
Dose: 10mg - 20mg PO (Never sublingual)
Pre-eclampsia. Use if Labetalol contraindicated (Asthma).
